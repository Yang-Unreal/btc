## 2. The On-Chain Fundamentals (The "What")
*Don't trust the price; trust the movement of coins.*

Before you buy, ask: **"Who is holding the chips?"**

### A. Accumulation vs. Distribution
*   **Bullish:** **Long-Term Holders (LTH)** are accumulating while price dips.
    *   *Meaning:* Smart money is buying from panicking retail.
*   **Bearish:** **LTHs** are selling into a rally.
    *   *Meaning:* Smart money is looking for exit liquidity.

### B. Valuation (MVRV Z-Score)
*   **The Buy Zone (Score < 0):** Everyone is underwater. Capitulation has happened. **Maximum Opportunity.**
*   **The Sell Zone (Score > 3.7):** Everyone is rich. Euphoria is peaking. **Maximum Risk.**

### C. Supply Shock (Exchange Reserves)
*   **Bullish:** Coins are leaving exchanges (moving to cold storage). Supply is vanishing.
*   **Bearish:** Coins are flooding onto exchanges. Whales are preparing to sell.

## 3. The Technical Trigger (The "Where")
*Precision matters. Don't catch a falling knife.*

### A. Trend Identification (The Filter)
*   **Weekly/Daily EMA:**
    *   **Price > EMA:** The trend is UP. **Longs only.**
    *   **Price < EMA:** The trend is DOWN. **Cash or Shorts.**

### B. Momentum Entry
*   **RSI Divergence (The Golden Signal):**
    *   **Bullish Divergence:** Price makes a Lower Low, but RSI makes a Higher Low. **STRONG BUY.**
    *   **Bearish Divergence:** Price makes a Higher High, but RSI makes a Lower High. **STRONG SELL.**

## 4. Risk Protocols (The Survival Guide)
*You cannot compound if you go to zero.*

### The Iron Laws
1.  **Stop Loss:** Mandatory. No trade exists without an invalidation point.
2.  **Position Size:** Risk **1-2%** of equity per trade. Never more.
3.  **Leverage:**
    *   **Spot:** Safe.
    *   **3x:** Aggressive.
    *   **>10x:** Gambling.
