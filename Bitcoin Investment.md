## 1. 游린 Market Mechanics (The Tools)

### Shorting & Derivatives
*   **Shorting Logic:** Borrow high $\rightarrow$ Sell $\rightarrow$ Buy back low $\rightarrow$ Return.
    *   *Risk:* **Unlimited.** If price doubles, you lose 100%. If it 10x, you owe 9x principal.
*   **Leverage:** A **Speed Amplifier**, not a win-rate booster.
    *   10x Leverage = 1% price move causes 10% equity change.
*   **Funding Rates:**
    *   **Positive (+):** Longs pay Shorts (Bullish sentiment/Greed).
    *   **Negative (-):** Shorts pay Longs (Bearish sentiment/Fear).

### 驕멆잺 Survival Rules (Non-Negotiable)
> **If you ignore these, you will go to zero.**
*   **Stop Loss:** Mandatory on every trade. No exceptions.
*   **Position Size:** Max risk **2%** of capital per trade.
*   **Leverage Cap:** Beginners **<3x**; Experts **<10x**.

### B. Technical Structure (Trend & Momentum)
*   **EMA (Trend):**
    *   Price > EMA = **Long Only**. Price < EMA = **Short Only**.
    *   *Golden Cross:* Fast EMA crosses above Slow EMA.
*   **RSI (Momentum):**
    *   **Divergence is King:** Price makes New High + RSI makes Lower High = **Top Reversal**.
    *   Price makes New Low + RSI makes Higher Low = **Bottom Reversal**.
*   **TD Sequential (Timing):**
    *   **9/13 Count:** Signals trend exhaustion. "Green 9" = Buy dip; "Red 9" = Sell top.
